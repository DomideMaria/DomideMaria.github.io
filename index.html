ABSTRACT.

My name is Maria Domide, and I recently graduated the Bachelor's course in Architecture at the University of Kent, United Kingdom. It was during middle school when my love for fine arts started to grow and take shape, motivating me to increase my knowledge base in the area and to firmly develop my graphic representational skills. I was not supported to further pursue my artistic drive, but right after I started high school, I discovered the field of Architecture that offered me the opportunity of exploiting both my technical and artistic side. Consequently, in the following years I saved my allowance to cover for summer drawing and Architecture classes, books and drawing materials, while spending all of my spare time exercising and self-studying, merging my passion for art with the technical skills acquired in school.

The following three years of academic training in the field of Architecture represented the first step in achieving both my career and personal goals, finding and improving myself. The Bachelor’s course provided me the tools and knowledge I needed to continue pursuing my passion. I am highly enthusiastic about working in the domain of Architecture as I firmly believe that when a person is engaged in what his keen interest blossoms, he will never feel tired; instead, he would feel motivated and have an enriching experience.
